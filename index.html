Welcome to Cards Kids Game

The ultimate educational app designed to make learning fun and interactive for kids!

Features:

Interactive Learning

Kids can explore and learn through interactive flashcards that cover essential educational topics such as numbers, letters, shapes, and fruits. Each card is designed with colorful graphics and engaging animations to capture children's attention and make learning enjoyable.

PeekaPoo Game

A unique game where kids can tap to reveal hidden fruits. Each tap reveals a random fruit emoji with its name, helping kids learn to identify different fruits in a fun way. The game includes a built-in timer to prevent rapid swiping, ensuring kids take their time to learn and recognize each fruit.

Voice Feedback

Integrated with a speech synthesizer, the app provides voice feedback for each card, helping kids with pronunciation and auditory learning. The voice feedback can be enabled or disabled based on user preferences, making it adaptable for different learning environments.

User-Friendly Design

The app features a clean and intuitive interface that is easy for kids to navigate. Large buttons and interactive elements ensure that children can use the app independently and confidently.

Safe and Secure

No ads, no in-app purchases, and no data collection ensure a safe and distraction-free learning experience for your kids. The app does not require any login or personal information, making it secure and straightforward to use.

Customizable Settings

Parents and educators can customize the app settings to suit the child's learning pace and needs. The app allows toggling sound feedback and adjusting the duration for which the fruit is revealed in the PeekaPoo game.

Why Choose Cards Kids Game?

Engaging and Educational: Combines fun and learning to keep kids engaged while educating them on essential topics.

Easy to Use: Designed with children in mind, ensuring they can navigate and use the app with minimal supervision.

Safe for Kids: Provides a secure environment with no ads or in-app purchases, allowing kids to focus on learning.
